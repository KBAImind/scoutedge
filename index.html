Install as App
💻 Laptop (Chrome / Edge)
  1. Open this file in Chrome or Edge
  2. Look for the install icon (⊕) in the address bar — far right
  3. Click it and select "Install"
  4. ScoutEdge opens as a standalone app with its own taskbar icon
📱 Android (Chrome)
  1. Open ScoutEdge URL in Chrome
  2. Tap the three-dot menu (⋮) top right
  3. Tap "Add to Home screen"
  4. Tap "Add" — icon appears on your home screen
📱 iPhone (Safari)
  1. Open ScoutEdge URL in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button at the bottom
  3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap "Add" — icon appears on your home screen
